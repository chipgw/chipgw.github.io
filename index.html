open source

I maintain a couple minor open source projects:

  • DepthView is a simple viewer for stereo 3d images.
  • DepthView2 a fancier viewer for 3d images and videos.
  • OpenBFME will someday be an RTS engine that can play BFME.
  • Photon is a game about lasers and mirrors and stuff.
  • Planets3D is a simple gravitational simulator.
  • Teensy Watch is a WIP watch program for the Teensy 3.1.
  • Planets3D, but in your browser with Emscripten.
  • Planets-JS is like the last one, but pure JS.
